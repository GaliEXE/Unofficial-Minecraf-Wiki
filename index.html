Welcome to the Unofficial Minecraft Wiki. The Purpose of this webpage is to tell you everything you need to know about Minecraft.


Games Created By Mojang
Minecraft: is an open world 3 Dimensional Sandbox game that allows you to roam a world made up of entities and blocks, the games divers gameplay allows for countless possibilities. Minecraft allows people to use their creativity on a completely new level.

There are currently three supported Official Minecraft Games: Minecraft Java Edition, Minecraft Bedrock Edition, and Minecraft Education Edition. In certain countries Minecraft has been changed in order to fit that countries specifications Four instance Minecraft China Edition (see. China Edition).
Minecraft Dungeons: is a Action RPG in where you are set into the world of Minecraft and must fight your way through generated levels to fight against the Arch Illager, the main villain.

Minecraft Dungeons is available on most platforms including PC, Nintendo Switch, PS5, and Xbox 1.
Games with Ended Support
Minecraft Story Mode Season 1 and Season 2: was a choose your own adventure video game, where you as the main character would pass through a story line and your choices would change the story. Season two is simply the storyline that takes place after Season one.
Minecraft Earth: Was a Mobile game that used AR (Altered Reality) to project the Minecraft world into the real one. You as the player would physically walk around gather resources, build on plots which could be viewed next to the real world on your mobile device. Minecraft Earth Was discontinued recently on June 30th, 2021.
